## 5. KPI DASHBOARD — FORMULAS & TARGETS

### Revenue KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Target | Frequency |
|-----|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Gross Revenue** | Sum of all marketplace sales | €850K/mo | Daily |
| **Net Revenue** | GR - Returns - Chargebacks | €750K/mo | Daily |
| **Revenue Growth** | (Current MoM - Prior MoM) / Prior MoM | +5-10%/mo | Monthly |
| **Revenue Mix** | Revenue by marketplace / Total Revenue | 60% Amazon, 15% eBay | Monthly |

### Margin & Profitability KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Target | Calculation |
|-----|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Gross Margin %** | (Net Rev - COGS) / Net Rev | >35% | (€750K - €300K) / €750K = 60% |
| **Contribution Margin %** | (GM - Direct Marketing) / Net Rev | >30% | (€450K - €60K) / €750K = 52% |
| **Operating Margin %** | EBIT / Net Revenue | >20% | €157.75K / €750K = 21.0% ✓ |
| **Net Profit Margin %** | Net Profit / Net Revenue | >12% | €111.94K / €750K = 14.9% ✓ |
| **EBITDA Margin %** | (EBIT + D&A) / Net Rev | >25% | (€157.75K + €5K) / €750K = 21.7% |

### Cost-of-Sales KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Target | Status |
|-----|---------|--------|--------|
| **COGS %** | COGS / Gross Revenue | <45% | €486.75K / €850K = 57.3% (includes all fulfillment) |
| **Marketplace Fee %** | (Commissions + Fees) / GR | <18% | €112.5K / €850K = 13.2% ✓ |
| **Fulfillment Cost %** | Logistics & Fulfillment / GR | <12% | €91K / €850K = 10.7% ✓ |
| **Payment Processing %** | Payment fees / Net Revenue | <2% | €11.25K / €750K = 1.5% ✓ |
| **TACoS (Total Ad Cost of Sales)** | Ad spend / Net Revenue | <5% | €13.1K / €750K = 1.75% ✓✓ |

### Customer & Inventory KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Target | Calculation |
|-----|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Inventory Turnover** | COGS / Avg Inventory | 18x/yr or 1.5x/mo | €180K / €120K = 1.5x/mo ✓ |
| **Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)** | 365 / Turnover Ratio | <20 days | 365 / 18 = 20.3 days |
| **Return Rate %** | Units Returned / Units Sold | <10% | Varies by marketplace: Amazon 8%, eBay 5%, Cdiscount 10% |
| **Chargeback Rate %** | Chargebacks / Gross Revenue | <1.5% | €15K / €850K = 1.76% (slightly high) |
| **Sell-Through Rate %** | Units Sold / Units Available | >80% | Monitor by SKU and marketplace |

### Cash Flow & Working Capital KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Target | Calculation |
|-----|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)** | DIO + DSO - DPO | <30 days | 20 + 5 - 30 = -5 days (POSITIVE) |
| **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)** | (AR / Revenue) × 365 | 3-5 days | (€180K / €750K) × 365 = 87.6 days (high due to marketplace batching) |
| **Days Payable Outstanding (DPO)** | (AP / COGS) × 365 | 30-45 days | (€95K / €486.75K) × 365 = 71.2 days (good terms) |
| **Working Capital** | Current Assets - Current Liabilities | >€100K | €545K - €165.5K = €379.5K ✓ |
| **Quick Ratio** | (CA - Inventory) / CL | >1.0 | (€545K - €120K) / €165.5K = 2.57 ✓✓ |

| KPI | Formula | Target | Notes |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|
