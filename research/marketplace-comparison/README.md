## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive competitive analysis of 20 European e-commerce marketplaces where Vendiamonoi.it currently operates or has expansion potential. The research covers 14 key dimensions across 20 platforms, generating 280+ comparative data points to inform strategic decision-making around resource allocation, category prioritization, and market expansion.

**Key Findings:**
- Commission structures vary by 8-25 percentage points across categories
- Payment terms range from 7-90 days, significantly impacting working capital
- Mirakl-based platforms (Kaufland, Cdiscount, Leroy Merlin, ManoMano) offer 40% faster integration
- Top 5 platforms by GMV (Amazon, eBay, Kaufland, Bol.com, Cdiscount) represent ~75% of addressable market
- Regional concentration risk: Germany (32% GMV), France (24%), Italy (18%)
- EU Digital Services Act compliance adds 3-8 hours monthly per marketplace
