## Key Sections at a Glance

**Section 1: CE Marking** — Mandatory for 25+ product categories; €5,000-€1,000,000 fines for violations

**Section 2: REACH** — 313 SVHCs to track; ≥0.1% threshold; SCIP database registration mandatory since Jan 2021

**Section 3: Product Regulations** — RoHS, WEEE, Batteries, Textiles, Toys, Food, Cosmetics all with distinct requirements

**Section 4: GPSR** — ALL products covered; Marketplace has active verification duty (not passive distributor)

**Section 5: Marketplace Programs** — Amazon requires Product Authenticity enrollment; eBay monitors Safety Gate daily

**Section 6: Claims** — 31 prohibited unfair practices; Green claims require third-party certification (new 2026)

**Section 7: IP** — EU exhaustion doctrine; selective distribution enforceable post-first sale

**Section 8: VAT** — IOSS mandatory for non-EU sellers; OSS mandatory for EU sellers >€10k revenue

**Section 9: EPR** — Registration mandatory in each country before first sale; €5,000-€100,000 penalties

**Section 10: Packaging** — Single-use plastic restrictions effective Jan 2025; recycled content targets 2030-2040
