## 6. SHARE OF VOICE & KEYWORD RANKING (COMPREHENSIVE)

### 6.1 SOV Definition & Calculation

**Share of Voice (SOV) — Core Formula:**
```
SOV (%) = Your Clicks / (Your Clicks + Competitors' Clicks) × 100

or

SOV (%) = Your Impressions / (Your Impressions + Competitors' Impressions) × 100
```

**SOV Calculation with Direct Competitor Set:**
```
Competitive_SOV = Your_Clicks / Sum(All_Competitor_Clicks) × 100
  [Isolates share against known competitors only]

Category_SOV = Your_Clicks / Category_Total_Clicks × 100
  [Broader view; includes all category competitors]
```

**Optimal SOV Targets by Market Position:**

| Market Position | SOV Target | Competitive Status | Action |
|-----------------|------------|-------------------|--------|
| Market Leader (Rank 1-3) | >30-40% | Dominant | Maintain/grow |
| Strong Contender (Rank 4-10) | 15-25% | Competitive | Grow toward 25% |
| Established Player (Rank 11-30) | 8-15% | Moderate | Grow toward 15% |
| Emerging Player (Rank 31+) | 3-8% | Weak | Grow toward 8-10% |

### 6.2 Keyword Elasticity & Saturation

**Keyword Elasticity to Position:**
```
Keyword_Elasticity = % Change in CTR / % Change in Position
```

**Elasticity Classification:**

```
High Elasticity (>2.5):
  - 1% position improvement → 2.5%+ CTR increase
  - Characteristics: Moderate competition, clear relevance ranking
  - Strategy: Position optimization has high ROI
  - Examples: Long-tail keywords, niche categories

Moderate Elasticity (1.5-2.5):
  - 1% position improvement → 1.5-2.5% CTR increase
  - Characteristics: Balanced competition, standard keywords
  - Strategy: Position important but not dominant factor
  - Examples: Category-level keywords, seasonal products

Low Elasticity (<1.5):
  - 1% position improvement → <1.5% CTR increase
  - Characteristics: High saturation, branded searches
  - Strategy: Position changes have minimal impact; focus on CTR improvement
  - Examples: Highly competitive keywords, branded searches
```

**Market Saturation Index:**
```
Saturation_Index = (Number_of_Results / Category_Volume) × 100

Saturation Levels:
- <5%: Low saturation (high elasticity; easy wins)
- 5-15%: Moderate saturation (moderate elasticity; balanced effort)
- 15-30%: High saturation (low elasticity; difficult positioning)
- >30%: Extreme saturation (minimal elasticity; shift strategy)
```

## 8. PROFITABILITY METRICS PER LISTING

### 8.1 Contribution Margin Analysis

**Base Contribution Margin Formula:**
```
Contribution_Margin = (Revenue - COGS - Variable_Costs) / Revenue × 100

Variable Costs Include:
- Fulfillment fees (FBA or shipping)
- Referral commission (Amazon: 6-45% by category)
- Payment processing
- Return logistics
```

**Example Calculation (Electronics, $100 COGS, $150 Selling Price):**
```
Revenue: $150
COGS: $100
FBA Fee (5%): $7.50
Amazon Referral (15%): $22.50
Payment Processing (2%): $3
Return Provision (3%): $4.50

Contribution Margin = ($150 - $100 - $7.50 - $22.50 - $3 - $4.50) / $150
                    = $12.50 / $150 = 8.3%
```

**Healthy Contribution Margin by Category:**

| Category | Target | Minimum | Warning |
|----------|--------|---------|----------|
| Electronics | 15-25% | >10% | <8% |
| Fashion | 35-50% | >25% | <20% |
| Home & Garden | 30-45% | >20% | <15% |
| Beauty | 45-60% | >35% | <25% |
| Sports & Outdoors | 35-50% | >25% | <20% |

### 8.2 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) & Payback Period

**CAC Calculation:**
```
CAC = Total_Marketing_Spend / New_Customers_Acquired

CAC_Payback_Period = CAC / Contribution_Margin_per_Unit
  [Number of units needed to recoup customer acquisition cost]
```

**Example:**
```
Marketing Spend (Month): $5,000
New Customers: 500
CAC: $5,000 / 500 = $10 per customer

Contribution Margin per Unit: $12.50
Payback Period: $10 / $12.50 = 0.8 units
  (i.e., customer breaks even after 0.8 units = 80% of first purchase)
```

**Healthy CAC Payback Targets:**
```
CACPayback < 1 unit: Excellent (customer profitable on first purchase)
CAC Payback 1-2 units: Good (customer profitable quickly)
CAC Payback 2-4 units: Acceptable (customer profitable within time frame)
CAC Payback >4 units: Concerning (long payback; depends on repeat purchase rate)
```

### 8.3 Portfolio Profitability & ROI

**Portfolio-Level Contribution Analysis:**
```
Portfolio_Total_Contribution = Σ(Revenue - COGS - Variable_Costs) for all SKUs

Portfolio_Margin = Portfolio_Total_Contribution / Total_Revenue × 100
```

**SKU-Level Ranking for Optimization:**

```
Tier 1 (High Volume + High Margin):
  - Contribution per SKU: >$5,000/month
  - Strategy: Protect; invest in growth
  - Action: Maximize visibility, maintain price

Tier 2 (High Volume + Low Margin OR Low Volume + High Margin):
  - Contribution per SKU: $1,000-$5,000/month
  - Strategy: Optimize; improve cost or volume
  - Action: Increase visibility or reduce COGS

Tier 3 (Low Volume + Low Margin):
  - Contribution per SKU: <$1,000/month
  - Strategy: Discontinue or restructure
  - Action: Consider discontinuing or bundling
```

## 10. FORMULAS & CALCULATIONS REFERENCE

### 10.1 Traffic & Visibility Formulas

**Click-Through Rate (CTR):**
```
CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × 100
Adjusted_CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × (1 - Scroll_Through_Rate)
Position_Adjusted_CTR = CTR / (1 + ln(Position))
CTR_Quality_Score = (Actual_CTR / Benchmark_CTR) × 100
```

**Share of Voice (SOV):**
```
SOV = (Your_Clicks / Total_Category_Clicks) × 100
Competitive_SOV = (Your_Clicks / Competitor_Total_Clicks) × 100
```

**Keyword Elasticity:**
```
Position_Elasticity = % Change in CTR / % Change in Position
Keyword_Elasticity = % Change in Rank / % Change in SEO_Score
```

### 10.2 Conversion & Sales Formulas

**Conversion Rate Variants:**
```
Unit_Session_Percentage (USP) = Units Ordered / Sessions
Order_Session_Percentage (OSP) = Orders / Sessions
Revenue_Conversion_Rate = Revenue / Sessions
Visits_to_Purchase = Orders / Unique_Visitors
```

**Revenue Calculations:**
```
Total_Revenue = Sessions × Conversion_Rate × Average_Order_Value
Adjusted_Revenue = Revenue - (Return_Rate × Revenue)
```

### 10.3 Quality & Performance Formulas

**Return Rate Variants:**
```
Unit_Return_Rate = (Units_Returned / Units_Ordered) × 100
Monetary_Return_Rate = (Revenue_from_Returns / Total_Revenue) × 100
Order_Return_Rate = (Orders_with_Returns / Total_Orders) × 100
Return_Velocity = (Units_Returned_Week1 / Total_Ordered) × 100
```

**Rating & Review Metrics:**
```
Weighted_Average_Rating = Σ(Rating × Count) / Total_Reviews
Review_Velocity = (Reviews_in_Period / Units_Sold_in_Period) × 100
Positive_Sentiment = (5★ + 4★ - 2★ - 1★) / Total_Reviews × 100
Negative_Sentiment = (2★ + 1★) / Total_Reviews × 100
```

**Buy Box Metrics:**
```
Buy_Box_Win_Rate = (Hours_with_Box / Total_Hours) × 100
Price_Competitiveness = (Your_Price / Lowest_Competitor_Price) × 100
```

### 10.4 Profitability Formulas

**Contribution Margin:**
```
Contribution_Margin = (Revenue - COGS - Variable_Costs) / Revenue × 100

Variable_Costs = Fulfillment_Fee + Referral_Fee + Payment_Processing + Return_Provision
```

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
```
CAC = Total_Marketing_Spend / New_Customers_Acquired
CAC_Payback = CAC / Contribution_Margin_per_Unit
CAC_ROI = (Lifetime_Value - CAC) / CAC × 100
```

**Portfolio Optimization:**
```
Portfolio_Contribution = Σ(Contribution_per_SKU) for all active SKUs
SKU_Efficiency = Contribution_per_SKU / COGS
Portfolio_ROI = Portfolio_Contribution / Total_Inventory_Value × 100
```

### 10.5 Trend & Forecasting Formulas

**Velocity Calculations:**
```
CTR_Velocity = (CTR_Current_Week - CTR_Previous_Week) / CTR_Previous_Week × 100
Revenue_Velocity = (Revenue_Current_Week - Revenue_Previous_Week) / Revenue_Previous_Week × 100
Growth_Momentum = Average_Velocity_Last_4_Weeks
```

**Trend Analysis:**
```
Linear_Trend = (Latest_4_Week_Average - Previous_4_Week_Average) / Previous_4_Week_Average × 100
Volatility_Index = Std_Dev(Daily_Values) / Mean(Daily_Values) × 100
```

**Forecasting:**
```
Projected_Revenue = Current_Revenue × (1 + Average_Growth_Rate)
Revenue_Impact_from_Position_Change = Current_Revenue × (1 + Elasticity × Position_Change %)
Break_Even_Analysis = Fixed_Costs / (Revenue_per_Unit - Variable_Cost_per_Unit)
```

### 10.6 Complete Formula Derivations with Examples

**Complex Profitability Model:**
```
Net_Profit = (Revenue × (1 - Return_Rate)) - COGS - Total_Variable_Costs - Marketing_Spend

Where:
  Revenue = Sessions × Conversion_Rate × Average_Order_Value
  Total_Variable_Costs = (Revenue × (1 - Return_Rate)) × (Fulfillment_Fee% + Referral_Fee% + Payment_Fee%)
  Marketing_Spend = CAC × Number_of_New_Customers

Example (Monthly):
  Sessions: 5,000
  Conversion Rate: 2.2%
  AOV: $95
  Revenue: 5,000 × 0.022 × $95 = $10,450
  Return Rate: 8%
  Net Revenue: $10,450 × (1 - 0.08) = $9,614
  
  COGS: 110 units × $50 = $5,500
  Fulfillment: 4% × $9,614 = $385
  Referral: 15% × $9,614 = $1,442
  Payment: 2% × $9,614 = $192
  Total Variable Costs: $2,019
  
  Marketing Spend: $200
  
  Net Profit: $9,614 - $5,500 - $2,019 - $200 = $1,895
  Net Margin: $1,895 / $10,450 = 18.1%
```

**Multi-Variable Sensitivity Analysis:**
```
Revenue_Sensitivity_to_Position = dRevenue/dPosition = Elasticity × Current_Revenue
Margin_Sensitivity_to_COGS = dMargin/dCOGS = -1 / (Revenue - COGS)
Profit_Sensitivity_to_CAC = dProfit/dCAC = -1 × (New_Customer_Count)

Example:
Revenue = $10,000
Elasticity = 2.2
Current Rank: 8

Revenue impact of 1-position improvement:
  = 2.2 × $10,000 × (1/8) ≈ $2,750 (+27.5% revenue)
```
