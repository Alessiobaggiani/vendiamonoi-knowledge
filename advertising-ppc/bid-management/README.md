## Key Metrics & Frameworks

- **Vickrey Auction**: Second-price bidding model showing €2.50 bid paying €1.81 actual CPC (27.6% discount)
- **Algorithmic Bidding Formula**: AOV × margin % × conversion rate = max CPC
- **Budget Allocation**: Top-down (€50K revenue × 28% ACoS = €14K budget) and bottom-up methods
- **Time-of-Day Optimization**: Amazon.de peaks at 12:00-13:00 (130 index) and 19:00-21:00 (140 index)
- **Seasonal Adjustment**: November +150%, December +135% index multipliers
