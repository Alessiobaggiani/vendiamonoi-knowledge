# Display & DSP Advertising Guide

## Overview

Comprehensive guide to display and demand-side platform (DSP) advertising across Amazon, Criteo, and European retail media networks, with coverage of video advertising and programmatic display strategy.

## Key Metrics & Benchmarks

- **Display CTR**: 0.3-0.8% (varies by audience type)
- **Sponsored Display ROAS**: 1.5-3.5x depending on vertical
- **Video completion rates**: 50-75% for in-stream ads
- **Frequency cap optimization**: 3-7 impressions per user per week
- **Viewability rate targets**: 70%+ for quality assurance

- Retargeting cart abandoners and past visitors
- Cross-device audience extension
- Video awareness campaigns on Twitch and OTT platforms
