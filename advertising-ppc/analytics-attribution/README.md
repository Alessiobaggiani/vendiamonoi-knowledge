# Domain 3.6: Analytics & Attribution

## Overview

Comprehensive guide to advertising performance reporting, attribution models, and advanced analytics across Amazon, eBay, Allegro, Bol.com, Kaufland, and Cdiscount platforms.

## Sections

1. **Advertising Performance Reporting** - Multi-marketplace reporting architecture, APIs, and data export
2. **Key Advertising Metrics** - Definitions, calculations, and benchmark analysis
3. **Attribution Models** - Last-click, first-click, multi-touch, view-through, and cross-device attribution
4. **Dashboards & BI Integration** - Dashboard design with Looker Studio, Tableau, Power BI, Qlik Sense
5. **Advanced Analytics** - Incrementality testing, market mix modeling, cannibalization analysis, cohort analysis
6. **Automation & Optimization** - Frameworks and machine learning optimization

## Key Metrics

- Impressions: Foundation metric for reach and viewability
- Clicks & CTR: Engagement indicators with platform-specific benchmarks
- CPC: Cost per click optimization across channels
- CVR: Conversion rate analysis and optimization
- ACoS: Advertising Cost of Sale (Amazon-specific but applicable framework)
- ROAS: Return on Ad Spend (critical for ROI analysis)
- TACoS: Total Advertising Cost of Sale (strategic metric)

## Benchmarks by Platform

- **Amazon Advertising**: CTR 0.3-0.8%, CPC $0.50-$2.00, ACoS 25-40%
- **eBay Promoted Listings**: CTR 0.2-0.6%, CPC $0.30-$1.50, ACoS 20-35%
- **Bol.com**: CTR 0.15-0.5%, CPC $0.20-$1.00, ACoS 18-30%
- **Allegro**: CTR 0.2-0.6%, CPC $0.25-$1.00, ACoS 20-35%
