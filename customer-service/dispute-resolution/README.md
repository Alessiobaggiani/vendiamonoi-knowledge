## Key Metrics & Targets

**Order Defect Rate (ODR):** <1% (Amazon threshold)
- 0.3-0.5%: Excellent
- 0.5-0.8%: Acceptable
- >1.0%: Account suspension risk

**eBay Defect Rate:** <1% (Top Rated requirement)
- Loss of Top Rated = €1,850/month in fee increases
- Buy Box impact: 75-85% revenue loss if suspended

**Chargeback Costs:** €20-50 per case + merchandise loss
- Prevention ROI: Signature requirement (€10) prevents €500+ losses

## Evidence Hierarchy (Most Reliable)

1. **Signed delivery proof** (90%+ win rate)
2. **Carrier tracking + photo** (85-90% win rate)
3. **QC documentation + warehouse photo** (75-80% win rate)
4. **Communication logs + offer response** (65-75% win rate)
5. **Listing screenshot + specifications** (60-70% win rate)

## Escalation Decision Tree

```
Claim <€50?
├─ YES → Refund, move on (cost of defense exceeds value)
└─ NO → Continue

Evidence = Signed delivery + tracking?
├─ YES → Defend, win rate 85-95%
└─ NO → Evaluate other evidence

Claim €50-300?
├─ YES → Offer partial refund (15-20%), settle most cases
└─ NO → Continue

Claim >€300?
├─ Strong evidence → Escalate to legal review
├─ Weak evidence → Partial refund or small loss
└─ Pattern fraud → Legal + chargeback monitoring
```

## Prevention Priorities (ROI Ranked)

## Annual Impact Example (500 Orders/Month)

**Without Prevention:**
- 6,000 orders/year × 0.8% dispute rate = 48 disputes
- Average loss: €300 per dispute = €14,400 losses
- Staff time: 48 cases × 4 hours = 192 hours (€2,880)
- Total: €17,280 annual cost

**With Full Prevention:**
- Same 6,000 orders × 0.2% dispute rate = 12 disputes
- Signature + photo investment: €50/month = €600/year
- Time reduction: 48 → 12 hours = €180 savings
- Net savings: €17,280 - €600 + €180 = **€16,860/year**
