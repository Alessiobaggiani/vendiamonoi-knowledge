# SECTION 1: FULFILLMENT MODEL COMPARISON

## Overview of Fulfillment Models in European Marketplaces

The European ecommerce marketplace ecosystem supports multiple fulfillment strategies, each with distinct advantages, costs, control levels, and scalability profiles. Successful sellers often employ hybrid approaches combining multiple models to optimize for different product categories, order volumes, and geographic coverage requirements.

The selection of fulfillment model represents one of the most critical operational decisions in marketplace selling, directly impacting cash flow, supplier relationships, customer satisfaction metrics, and profitability. This section provides detailed mechanics of each approach with exact specifications and comparative analysis.
