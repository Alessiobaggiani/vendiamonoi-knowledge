## SECTION 2: SHIPPING COST ESTIMATION

### 2.1 CARRIER PRICING FUNDAMENTALS

#### 2.1.1 Dimensional Weight Formula (Universal Standard)

**Formula:** (Length cm × Width cm × Height cm) ÷ 5000 = Dimensional Weight (kg)

**When Applied:**
- When dimensional weight exceeds actual weight
- Chargeable weight = MAXIMUM(Actual Weight, Dimensional Weight)

**Example Calculation:**
- Package: 40cm × 30cm × 20cm, actual weight 2 kg
- Dimensional weight: (40 × 30 × 20) ÷ 5000 = 24,000 ÷ 5000 = 4.8 kg
- Chargeable weight: 4.8 kg (dimensional exceeds actual)
- Carrier bills based on 4.8 kg, not 2 kg

#### 2.1.2 Weight Step Pricing (Standard for EU Carriers)

Most EU carriers use weight steps. Shipper billed for lowest step that meets or exceeds weight:

| Weight Range (kg) | DHL | GLS | PostNL | Correos |
|-----------------|-----|-----|--------|----------|
| 0.5 - 1.0 | €5.50 | €4.80 | €5.20 | €5.10 |
| 1.1 - 2.0 | €6.80 | €5.95 | €6.40 | €6.35 |
| 2.1 - 3.0 | €8.10 | €7.10 | €7.60 | €7.60 |
| 3.1 - 5.0 | €9.90 | €8.60 | €9.15 | €9.20 |
| 5.1 - 10.0 | €13.50 | €11.40 | €12.30 | €12.60 |
| 10.1 - 15.0 | €17.80 | €15.10 | €16.20 | €16.90 |
| 15.1 - 20.0 | €22.40 | €19.00 | €20.40 | €21.50 |

## SECTION 3: WEIGHT & DIMENSIONAL WEIGHT

### 3.1 AMAZON FBA SIZE TIER DEFINITIONS (Exact Specifications)

#### 3.1.1 Small Standard-Size (SSS)

**Dimensional Limits:**
- Length ≤ 15" (38.1 cm)
- Width ≤ 12" (30.5 cm)
- Height ≤ 0.75" (1.9 cm)

**Weight Limit:** 0.5 kg (1.1 lbs)

**Fee Impact:** Lowest FBA fulfillment fees (€1.30-€1.50 range). Preferred tier for high-volume, low-margin products.

**Examples:** Books (paperback), small electronics accessories, phone cases, watch bands, light apparel.

#### 3.1.2 Large Standard-Size (LSS)

**Dimensional Limits (all must be true):**
- Length ≤ 18" (45.7 cm)
- Width ≤ 14" (35.6 cm)
- Height ≤ 8" (20.3 cm)

**Weight Limit:** 0.5 kg - 20 kg (1.1 - 44 lbs)

**Weight-Based Fees (Germany Example, Feb 2026):**

| Weight | Fee per Unit |
|--------|-------------|
| 0.5-1 kg | €2.50 |
| 1-2 kg | €3.20 |
| 2-5 kg | €4.10 |
| 5-10 kg | €5.80 |
| 10-15 kg | €7.40 |
| 15-20 kg | €8.90 |

**Examples:** Most household items, textiles, toys, small furniture, electronics under 20kg.

#### 3.1.3 Small Oversize (SO)

**Definition:** Items exceeding standard dimensions but not reaching large oversize threshold.

**Dimensional Qualifiers (any of these trigger SO):**
- Length > 18" OR
- Width > 14" OR
- Height > 8" (for items under 3kg)

**Weight Limit:** Up to 3 kg (6.6 lbs)

**Base Fee Structure (Germany, Feb 2026):**

| Weight | Base Fee | Per kg Add-on | Total Example (2.5kg) |
|--------|----------|--------------|----------------------|
| Per unit | €4.50 | €0.75 | €6.38 |

**Examples:** Large picture frames, oversized clothing boxes, small furniture, kitchen appliances.

#### 3.1.4 Large Oversize (LO)

**Definition:** Large items beyond standard dimensions, 3kg+

**Dimensional Qualifier:** Longest side > 18" OR median side > 14" OR shortest side > 8" (for items > 3kg)

**Weight Limit:** 3 kg - 30 kg (6.6 - 66 lbs)

**Simplified Fee Structure (Feb 2026 - changed from 28 to 17 bands):**

New structure = Base Rate + Per-Kilogram Incremental Charge

| Country | Base (€) | Per kg (€) | 10kg Example |
|---------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Germany | €7.20 | €1.10 | €18.20 |
| France | €7.80 | €1.25 | €19.30 |
| Italy | €7.50 | €1.15 | €18.80 |
| Spain | €7.65 | €1.20 | €19.65 |

**Previous Structure (Pre-Feb 2026):** Had 28 separate weight-size bands. Amazon unified to reduce complexity.

**Examples:** Large furniture, sports equipment, outdoor gear, appliances, construction tools.

### 3.2 DIMENSIONAL WEIGHT IMPACT BY MARKETPLACE

#### 3.2.1 Amazon FBA Dimensional Weight Rule

**Formula:** (Length cm × Width cm × Height cm) ÷ 5000

**Applied to:** All parcel and oversize tiers. Seller pays fee based on higher of actual weight or dimensional weight.

**Example Scenario:**
- Product: Office chair, 70cm × 70cm × 90cm (assembled), actual weight 8 kg
- Dimensional weight: (70 × 70 × 90) ÷ 5000 = 441,000 ÷ 5000 = 88.2 kg
- Chargeable weight: 88.2 kg (far exceeds actual weight)
- Fee: €88.20 × €1.10/kg (oversize) = SEVERE oversize penalty
- **Outcome:** Large oversize tier would reject this item; requires disassembly/box optimization

**Critical Insight:** Packaging density directly impacts FBA viability. Bulky items with low weight face effective size penalties.

#### 3.2.2 eBay Dimensional Weight

eBay uses dimensional weight for shipping cost calculation if:
- Dimensional weight > actual weight by more than threshold
- Applies to items fulfillment shipping cost estimate

**Formula:** Same (L × W × H ÷ 5000)

**Impact:** Affects buyer shipping costs shown at checkout. High dimensional weight = higher displayed shipping cost = lower conversion rates.

#### 3.2.3 Bol.com Dimensional Weight

Bol.com uses dimensional weight for their fulfillment service (Bol.com fulfillment):

**Formula:** (Length cm × Width cm × Height cm) ÷ 5000

**Applied to:** Bol.com fulfillment by Bol services.

**Chargeable Weight:** Maximum of actual weight or dimensional weight.

### 3.3 SIZE TIER OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES

#### 3.3.1 Packaging Optimization Decision Tree

**Question 1: Is dimensional weight > 1.5× actual weight?**
- YES → Repackage with void fill removed or use flat box
- NO → Proceed to Q2

**Question 2: Does product cross size tier boundary?**
- Product at 17cm height crossing from SSS (≤15cm) to LSS?
- YES → Reduce packaging or reposition
- NO → Proceed to Q3

**Question 3: Is product in LSS tier with weight 15-20kg?**
- YES → Consider fragmentation (multiple smaller units) if possible
- NO → Optimize shipping method instead of packaging

#### 3.3.2 Fee Impact Examples (Packaging Changes)

**Example 1: Clothing Item**
- Original: Bulky box 40×35×20cm, 1.2kg actual
- Dimensional weight: (40×35×20)÷5000 = 5.6 kg
- SSS tier impossible (height exceeds 0.75")
- LSS tier fee (Germany): €4.10 (at 5.6kg chargeable weight)
- **Optimization:** Use compression vacuum bag, reduce to 25×25×10cm, 1.2kg actual
- New dimensional: (25×25×10)÷5000 = 1.25 kg
- New fee (Germany LSS 1-2kg): €2.50
- **Savings:** €1.60 per unit (39% reduction)

**Example 2: Electronics Item**
- Original: Hard case 50×40×25cm, 2.5kg actual
- Dimensional weight: (50×40×25)÷5000 = 10 kg
- LSS tier fee (Germany, 10kg band): €5.80
- **Optimization:** Remove excess case void fill, use minimal foam. 45×35×22cm, 2.5kg actual
- New dimensional: (45×35×22)÷5000 = 7.0 kg
- New fee (Germany LSS, 5-10kg): €4.10
- **Savings:** €1.70 per unit (29% reduction)

### 3.4 OVERSIZE PENALTIES & THRESHOLDS

#### 3.4.1 Special Oversize (SO) Category

**Automatic Triggers if ANY of these apply:**
- Weight > 20 kg (44 lbs) AND longest dimension > 18" OR
- Longest dimension > 60" OR
- Longest + girth > 130" (where girth = 2×width + 2×height)

**Minimum Referral Fee (Effective Feb 2026):** €20 per unit (down from €25)

**Triggers Automatic SO Categorization:**
- Large furniture (beds, sofas, wardrobes)
- Appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers)
- HVAC equipment
- Outdoor structures (gazebos, sheds, trampolines)

#### 3.4.2 Weight-Based Fee Structure (Large Oversize Example - Germany)

|------------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| 20.1-30kg | €7.20 | €1.10 | €33.20 |

**Structure Note:** Base fee remains constant; per-kg charge applied linearly. Unlike previous 28-band system, new structure provides cost transparency and linear cost scaling.
